Br

The Br HTML element produces a line break in text. It is useful for writing a poem or an address, where the division of lines is significant. To code for this element is <br> :

This element is a stand alone element and doesn't need to be closed

Example:

When I was just a little girl
I asked my mother, what will I be
Will I be pretty, will I be rich
Here's what she said to me
